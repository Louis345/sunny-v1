Today we are measuring the same pencil in two different ways. The pencil does not change. Only the size of our measuring steps changes.

Centimeters are small steps, so it takes more of them to cover the pencil. Inches are bigger steps, so it takes fewer of them to cover the same pencil.

Before we reveal the answer, make a prediction: if the measuring step gets smaller, will the number of steps go up or down?

Ready? The number goes up. Smaller units make bigger counts. Bigger units make smaller counts. Same pencil, different measuring steps.
