**The Best Day Ever**

Today was a very special day. Many visitors came to our school area. 

First, a farmer brought fresh corn. Then a baker shared sweet bread pieces. A doctor showed us her tools. She taught us to measure our height.

Next, an actor made us laugh. A sailor told short, true ocean stories. A dancer moved for two hours! 

Our teacher asked us to notice everything. A writer took notes in order. Last came an inventor with cool gadgets.

"This is the best day ever!" said Emma. All the visitors smiled and waved goodbye. We learned so much today.
