# Reina — Curriculum Plan

## Current Focus: Math — Addition & Subtraction

Reina has declared she wants to excel at math. Starting with addition and subtraction diagnostic.

### Phase 1 — Diagnostic (Sessions 1-3)
Goal: Find her floor and ceiling. Cover all buckets.
- Session 1: Single digit (2+3, 5+4, 8+1) and teens minus small (12-3, 15-4)
- Session 2: Crossing 10 (8+5, 7+6, 9+4) and larger subtraction (18-9, 16-7)
- Session 3: Double digit easy (24+5, 37-10, 45+11)

### Phase 2 — Strengthen Weak Spots (Sessions 4-6)
`mathProblem` tool will identify the weak bucket after Phase 1.
Focus 80% of problems in that bucket, 20% review of mastered range.

### Success Metrics
- 90%+ accuracy in a bucket = mastered, move up
- Below 70% after 5 attempts = slow down, scaffold smaller

### Dopamine Loop Rules
- Every correct answer = celebrate + 2-3 turns of banter
- 3 in a row = streak callout
- 5 in a row = end with something like "That's a PIN, Reina. Undefeated."
- Wrong answer = never shame, always scaffold to a win
