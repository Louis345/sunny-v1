# Activity Capability Audit

`src/engine/activityToolCatalog.ts` is the machine-readable source of truth for activity capability cards. This document is the current human-readable snapshot for the first five audited baseline activities.

| Activity | What It Measures | Real Difficulty Levels | Key Config Knobs | Signals Emitted | Signals Missing | Psychologist Guidance |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Word Radar | Visible-word recognition, read-aloud flow, recall practice only when the answer is hidden. | `visible_read`, `partial_visual_recall`, `hidden_word_recall`. | `recallMode`, `inputMode`, `showTimer`, `timerSeconds`, `hideWordDuringResponse`, `requiresCapturedResponse`. | Per-word response when captured, hit/miss, timer pressure, retry/skip. | Independent mastery when the word is visible, reliable mastery without captured response, comprehension. | Use visible mode for warmup and confidence. Escalate only after strong evidence. Do not treat visible mode as mastery. |
| Pronunciation | Reading/pronunciation fluency, decoding struggle, hesitation, support requests, recovery, flow tolerance. | `supported_read_aloud`, `flow_replay_expansion`, `diagnostic_reading_probe`. | `replayMode`, `harderReplayGrowth`, `dosagePolicy`, `supportMode`, `chunkHints`, `sfxProfile`, `pace`, `cohortSize`. | Word start, hit/miss, support cue, replay selection, completion accuracy. | Written spelling mastery, comprehension beyond decoding, exact phonics error type without richer capture. | Choose for reading/pronunciation practice. Slow and scaffold after frustration. Let strong mastery expand dosage without making it feel like a grind. |
| Spell Check | Spelling construction from hidden/audio/context prompt, letter order, retry recovery. | `guided_letter_build`, `audio_prompt_spell`, `cold_recall_spell`. | `promptMode`, `hideTargetWord`, `maxRetries`, `letterBankMode`, `perTargetResults`, `hintPolicy`. | Per-target correctness, attempt text, retry count, completion accuracy. | Independent mastery when visible answers/retries/hints happen first, reading fluency, frustration timing unless emitted. | Use after warmup for cleaner spelling evidence. Hinted/retry-heavy runs are practice. Avoid if isolated letter selection is frustrating. |
| Monster Stampede | Fast orthographic recognition, spelling under pressure, recovery after misses, competition/streak engagement. | `visible_stampede`, `targeted_recovery_run`, `pressure_probe`. | `speedMode`, `cohortSize`, `wordOrder`, `visibleWordMode`, `missPenalty`, `streakRewards`. | Hit/miss, streaks, recovery after miss, completion/score. | Clean first-pass baseline, paper transfer, mastery with visible/retry support. | Use for flow practice after misses are known. Great for confidence and speed; practice, not mastery. |
| Wheel of Fortune | Pattern inference, letter strategy, board-state reasoning, choice/surprise engagement. | `mystery_reward_word`, `pattern_inference`, `strategy_challenge`. | `puzzleSource`, `hintPolicy`, `wheelRisk`, `opponentMode`, `targetWordCount`, `rewardTiming`. | Letter guesses, board state, solve result, coins/reward outcome. | Full-list cold spelling mastery, pronunciation, transfer without follow-up. | Use as mystery/reward or pattern inference after evidence-generating work. Excellent preference signal; not mastery by itself. |
